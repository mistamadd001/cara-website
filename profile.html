Cara is a South Australian glass artist specialising in both blown and cast forms. She studied at UniSA's School of Art, Architecture and Design between 2012-2015 in the Bachelor of Visual Arts specialising in Glass. In 2015 she was offered a partial scholarship to attend the Pilchuck school of Glass summer programme 'Digital Playground' taught by Norwood Viviano.


In 2016 Cara hopes to complete her BaVA Honours again specialising in Glass after which she hopes to secure a position in The Jam Factory's mentorship programme
